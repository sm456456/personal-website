001 : Share Your Meal

Share Your Meal is an app that was created as a response to the lockdown. The initial idea was to help people to socialise with others during their meal time. To have a casual chat and company during the Corona times and hopefully after that too.

Used tech stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, React-bootstrap, Node.JS, MongoDB, Mongoose, Socket.IO, Simple-Peer, etc.
