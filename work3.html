003 : Pokemon FightClub

Entertaining full stack app. A user can choose one of dynamically loaded Pokemon, the opponent will be chosen randomly and then they go to the arena. The points for each pokemon change after every attack. Firework will display if a user winns. After the game, a user can choose another Pokemon and fight again. The leader board tracks down each fight.

Used tech stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Bootstrap, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, etc.
