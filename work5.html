001 : Share Your Meal

Share Your Meal is an app that was created as a response to the lockdown. People were lonely and were missing to socialise with others during their meal time.

Used tech stack: HTML, CSS, React, React-bootstrap, Node.JS, MongoDB, Mongoose, Socket.IO, Simple-Peer, etc.
